Power BI Projects

This is a Pizza Sales visualization project transaction data from Plato's Pizza. Plato is a Greek-inspired pizza place in New Jersey. The dataset has information of over 49k orders with a total revenue of over $815k in 2015.Highest size sold was Large, while XXL was the worst sold size. Fridays had the highest sales of the week as this is the end of a working week. More promos could be generated on other categories of Pizza to attract more sales.

This is a Mexican restaurant and the data contains 5 CSV files. Mexixan cuisine a popular delicacy and also called "RESTAURANTE". The dataset has information of 4 states with 112 various cuisines on a total of 138 customers with medium delicacy prices.Smoking was prohibited in most of the restaurants, whereas, Young Adults age bracket had the highest number of smokers and drinkers.Average serving speed rate was 1.09 mins which means they had a lean approach to their customers.

This project is a visualization a Call Center. Total calls of 5000 were handled by 8 call agents at an average speed of 54.75 secs. January recorded a sharp trend of calls while March was the lowest. In retrospect, there were more answered calls than unanswered calls. Agent Jim answered the highest number of calls whereas Stewart handled the least. 5 Call topics were recorded, Admin support recording the highest.

This project is a visualization of transaction data from OList. OList is the largest department store in Brazil. The dataset has information on 90k orders from 2016 to 2018 made at multiple marketplaces in Brazil.
